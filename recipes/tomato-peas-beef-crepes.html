Tomato, Peas and Beef Crèpes

Description

A savory version of crèpes!

Ingredients

For the batter:

  • 2 eggs
  • 120ml milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 30g butter
  • 120g flour

For the topping:

  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 can of peas
  • 250g of ground beef
  • 200g onion
  • Eggs (one for each crèpe)
  • 250g grated cheese of your choice
  • Tomato paste
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Steps

Batter:

  1. Mix together eggs, milk and a pinch of salt.
  2. Melt butter in microwave, stir into batter.
  3. Gradually mix in flour. Batter should be very runny/liquid!

Topping:

  1. Pre-heat pan and add oil.
  2. Finely chop tomatoes.
  3. Cook onion for 2-3 minutes, then add beef and cook for 2-3 more minutes.
  4. Add tomato paste, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper (and optionally other spices as desired) to onion and beef.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes. Finally, add peas and cook for 2-3 more minutes.

Crèpes:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 100°C.
  2. Heat up crèpe iron to high heat.
  3. Pour a ladle-full of batter on crèpe iron, cook briefly and flip. Reduce heat.
  4. At low heat, sprinkle on grated cheese, making sure to leave a hole in the center and covering the rim of the crèpe. Fold edges of crèpe to achieve rectangular form.
  5. Add egg into center of crèpe. Increase heat.
  6. Arrange tomato-peas-beef mixture on crèpe. Cook roughly 5 minutes, to ensure egg cooks as much as possible while on crèpe iron.
  7. Place crèpe into pre-heated oven, and repeat steps with next crèpe.
  8. Let crèpes continue to bake in oven until topping is nicely baked.
